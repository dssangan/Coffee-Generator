Coffee Info



Not sure what those fancy names mean?

We're here to help!


Macchiato:

Espresso is poured on top instead of being stirred into the drink.

Americano:

Made from espresso and water only.

Frappuccino:

Blended drinks, typically made with coffee. Often come with whipped cream and drizzle.

Latte:

Made with steamed milk and shots of espresso, with any flavor of milk, and can be hot or cold.

Mocha:

Are like lattes, they are made of espressos and milk, but tend to include chocolate.

Cappuccino:

Made solely of steamed milk and espresso, but tend to be mainly comprised of foam.
